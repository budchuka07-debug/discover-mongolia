About Visit Mongolia Tours

Travel information created by a Mongolian tour guide with real field experience.

Our Story

Visit Mongolia Tours was created based on real travel and tourism experience.

The founder of this website has traveled to more than 20 countries and over 50 cities around the world — both independently and through international tour companies.

For the past 10 years, the founder has worked in Mongolia’s tourism industry as a tour guide and camp chef, working directly with international visitors from different countries.

Through these experiences, we realized that many travelers want reliable and simple information about traveling in Mongolia — including destinations, tours, accommodations, and local experiences.

That is how the idea for this website was born.

Our Mission

Our goal is to help travelers discover Mongolia in an easier and more comfortable way by providing helpful travel information, recommended routes, tours, and booking options.

We aim to connect travelers with Mongolia’s beautiful nature, nomadic culture, and unique travel experiences.
