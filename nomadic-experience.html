Real Nomadic Experience in Mongolia

Stay with a local herder family and experience authentic Mongolian life.

Live Like a Nomad

This is not a hotel experience. You will stay with a real Mongolian nomadic family and experience their daily life, culture and traditions.

What You Will Experience

  • Milk cows, goats or camels
  • Learn to make traditional dairy products
  • Ride horses or camels
  • Live in a traditional ger (yurt)
  • Enjoy homemade Mongolian food

Real Local Family

We work with real nomadic families with 5 types of livestock and even hundreds of camels. This is a true cultural experience.

Comfort Options

You can combine this experience with tourist camps that include proper toilets and showers if needed.
