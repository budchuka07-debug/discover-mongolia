Transport in Mongolia

Travel in Mongolia can include buses, domestic flights, trains and private 4x4 vehicles.

Bus

Buses connect Ulaanbaatar with many provinces, but comfort and schedules vary.

Train

Train routes are limited but useful for certain destinations and cross-country travel.

Domestic Flights

Flights save time on long routes such as Murun, Dalanzadgad or Ulgii.
